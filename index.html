"Generate kindness, knowledge, and beauty wherever we go."



I love music and math. I spent a decade in the systems world, training and designing systems for IBM in New York and Bank of America and Charles Schwab in San Francisco.

I then composed music and songs for films for NOVA, PBS, and the History Channel. Currently I work in both fields, teaching and writing code and composing documentary songs with people.

As for schooling, I earned a BA at Columbia, an MBA at Berkeley, and a PhD at Prescott. These days I study online at freecodecamp.com and live in Rockport, Maine, with my dear wife Barbara.

— Malcolm Brooks
